ABOUT US

Who We Are

We are a group of passionate individuals who are committed to reducing the grave problem of food wastage. We believe that change comes from within and that is why we are trying to create awareness among people regarding the dire situation of food wastage which currently persists all over the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help create awareness among the people concerning the alarming levels of food wastage and the repercussions that follow this situation if the current state persists. We aim to not only educate people regarding this problem but also provide them with convenient solutions which they can implement in their own homes, for instance, providing them with recipes that can be cooked with leftover food items to reduce food wastage.

Our Team

Our team is made up of university students with a strong desire to do some good in this world by bringing the attention of the people towards a concerning problem of the world by utilizing the skills which we have gained through our education.
