Delicious Recipes You can make with leftovers!

Vegetable Stir Fry

Transform leftover veggies into a flavorful stir fry in minutes!!

Ingredients:

  • Leftover vegetables and protein (optional)
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ginger, minced or grated
  • Soy sauce or tamari
  • Sesame oil
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cooked rice or noodles (optional)
Instructions: Heat oil in a skillet or wok. Sauté garlic and ginger. Add leftover vegetables, stir-fry until tender. Add protein (if using), stir. Season with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper. Cook until heated through. Serve hot over rice or noodles.

Chicken Soup

Turn yesterday's roast chicken into a hearty soup for a cozy night in.

Ingredients:

  • Leftover cooked chicken, shredded or chopped
  • Leftover vegetables (carrots, celery, onions)
  • Chicken broth
  • Garlic, minced
  • Olive oil
  • Salt, pepper
  • Herbs (optional)
Instructions: Sauté garlic, vegetables in olive oil until soft. Add chicken, broth. Season with salt, pepper, herbs. Simmer for 10-15 mins. Add leftover rice/pasta (optional). Serve hot.
